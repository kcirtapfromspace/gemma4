## What we tried, what didn't work (defensible negative results)

3 LLM-tuning levers cleanly killed with data:

- **Fast-path threshold sweep** (Cand A) — F1 flat across 0.5–0.9; threshold
  has zero effect on this bench because all fast-path RAG hits score ≥ 1.0.
- **Self-consistency / temperature sampling** (Cand B) — single n=1 temp=0.2
  recovery on strep was statistical noise (0/5 reproduces); knowledge gap
  on adv4 LOINCs is not sampling-fixable.
- **System-prompt A/B** (Cand C) — terse variant 22% faster on adv4 but
  regresses precision on combined-27 from 1.000 → 0.944 with 4 FPs.
  Prescriptive prompt is load-bearing.

These belong in the submission narrative as "what we ruled out." They
prove the F1 = 1.000 claim is the result of pipeline engineering
(deterministic + RAG + Cand D gate + lookup curation), NOT model tuning.
